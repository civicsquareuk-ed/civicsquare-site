About the project

Why Civic Square exists

Project Civic Square was founded in 2025 to tackle one of the biggest problems in British society: young people feeling disillusioned with democracy.

Compared with older generations, young people are more likely to prefer unelected leaders to democracy, and more dissatisfied with the state of democracy itself.

We use proven strategies to help young people reflect and engage with ideas on British Values, democracy, freedoms, the rule of law, and authoritarianism.

The issue is pressing

Young people can vote at age 16 — yet schools are not well equipped to help them navigate their new power and rights.

Our theory of change

Make democracy education enjoyable for teachers. Inspire students through impactful testimonies. Build youth participation and democratic resilience.
